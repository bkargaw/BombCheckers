Game Rules

The game is the same as regular checkers with a couple of twists... all pieces behave like classic checker piece but with the following exceptions:
  • Bomb pieces will explode when they capture a piece; during the explosion any piece within one grid space will die.
  • Shield pieces are impervious to any explosion.

Moves

- right Mouse click to select a piece or empty square (Note:- only need to select a piece once a turn)

- press spacebar or return to end a turn
