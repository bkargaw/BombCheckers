Game Rules

The game is the same as regular checkers with a couple of twists... all pieces behave like classic checker piece but with the following exceptions
  • Bomb pieces will explode when they capture a piece; during the explosion any piece within one gird space will die
  • Shield pieces behave like classic piece but will be impervious to any explosion.

Moves

- Mouse click to selet a piece or empty square (Note:- only need to select a piece once a turn)
- Spacebar to end a trun
